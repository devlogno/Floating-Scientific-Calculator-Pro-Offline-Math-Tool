# Privacy Policy for Floating Scientific Calculator Pro: Offline Math Tool.

I value your privacy and believe in full transparency. This Privacy Policy describes how I handle information in the Floating Scientific Calculator Pro: Offline Math Tool. for Chrome.

## Data Collection
I collect no data. 
- I do not track your usage.
- I do not collect your personal information.
- I do not monitor your calculations.
- I do not use any external analytics or tracking cookies.

## Data Storage
All your data, including your calculation history and window settings, is stored locally on your own device using Chrome's local storage API. I have no access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers.

## Third-Party Access
Since I collect no data, I have nothing to share with third parties. I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties.

## Offline Use
The Floating Scientific Calculator Pro: Offline Math Tool. is designed to work entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection to perform calculations.

## Changes to This Policy
If I decide to change this privacy policy, I will update this page. However, my commitment to collecting no data will remain the core of this project.

## Contact
If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can contact me through the official project repository.
